Agile 8 — Pre-Session Mobility

Do these before every session. Also recommended on rest days. Takes 10–20 minutes.

FOAM ROLLING (hold tight spots)

IT Band

Lie on your side on top of the foam roller. Slide up and down from your knee to just above your hip.

Piriformis

Sit on the foam roller, cross your leg onto the opposite knee and lean onto the side of that knee. Roll up and down across your glute.

Adductor

With belly to the ground, place the inside of your thigh on the foam roller and roll around.

DYNAMIC DRILLS

V-Sit Roll Over

Sit with legs in front of you, roll back until toes touch the ground behind your head. Roll forward and spread legs in a straddle.

Hip Rotation

Palms and knees on the ground. Keep torso stable and trace circles with one knee. Both sides.

Groiner

Sink into a deep lunge with hands on the ground, forward foot outside your hand. Jump and switch legs.

Frog Jump

Start in push up position, jump both feet forward to land outside your hands. Reverse and repeat.

First Baseman Stretch

Lunge and touch your knee to the ground. Raise the arm on the same side as the grounded knee, lean forward to stretch your hip flexor.
